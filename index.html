About

Oakcrest Builders is a family-based company and has been building quality new custom single family homes. Headquartered in Mendon, Massachusetts, Oakcrest Builders has an outstanding portfolio with a focus on quality materials and customizing details that separate their homes from the competition. With our unmatched development and construction expertise, integrity, craftsmanship, and customer service we can work with you to create a truly customized home that meets your needs. We want to ensure your satisfaction when it comes to design, construction, and finishes, as we know that investing in a home is one of the biggest and most important decisions you'll ever make. We are here to guide you, answer questions, and make sure the process goes as smoothly and effortlessly as possible.

Robert Roach, owner of Oakcrest Builders, has over 40 years of experience in excavating and building. Starting his own excavation business while still in college, Mr. Roach relocated to Massachusetts to work under a master builder. During this time, he performed excavations and building support that were featured on the show “This Old House.” As owner of Oakcrest Builders, Mr. Roach provides impeccable attention to detail and energy efficiency, as well as responsiveness and customer support.
